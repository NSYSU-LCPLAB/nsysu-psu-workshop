About the Workshop

Three Years of Bridging
Two Continents

What began as a landmark MOU signing between Penn State College of Engineering and NSYSU has evolved into one of the most anticipated bilateral workshops in semiconductor and photonics research.

Now returning to Kaohsiung for its third edition, the workshop brings together distinguished faculty from both institutions, industry leaders from Taiwan's semiconductor ecosystem, and emerging researchers.

本次研討會延續 NSYSU 與 PSU 之學術交流精神，以三年為基礎，深化雙邊合作，探討半導體與光電領域之最新突破，並促進產學界的實質對話與合作機會。

Workshop History
2024

1st Edition — Kaohsiung, Taiwan

Inaugural workshop at NSYSU following the PSU–NSYSU MOU signing. 9 PSU professors, NSYSU faculty & industry leaders.

2025

2nd Edition — University Park, Pennsylvania

Moved to Penn State, expanding collaboration with joint sessions and lab visits.

2026

3rd Edition — Back to Kaohsiung ✦

Returning to NSYSU with expanded scope and deeper industry engagement.

Workshop Topics

Key Research Themes

01

Advanced Semiconductor Packaging

Heterogeneous integration, chiplet architectures, 3DIC, and wafer-level packaging.

02

Silicon Photonics & Integrated Optics

On-chip optical interconnects and high-bandwidth communication for AI accelerators.

03

Optical Computing & Neuromorphic Systems

Photonic neural networks and optical reservoir computing for energy-efficient AI.

04

Wide-Bandgap & Power Semiconductors

GaN, SiC devices for power electronics, RF, and 6G applications.

05

Photonics Sensing & Imaging

Polarimetric imaging, LiDAR, and advanced sensing for autonomous vehicles.

06

Metasurfaces & Reconfigurable Optics

Flat optics, liquid crystal RIS, and dynamic beam steering for 6G and AR/VR.
