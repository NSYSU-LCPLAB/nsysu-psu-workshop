About the Workshop

Three Years of Bridging
Two Continents

What began as a landmark MOU signing between Penn State College of Engineering and NSYSU has evolved into one of the most anticipated bilateral workshops in semiconductor and photonics research.

Now returning to Kaohsiung for its third edition, the workshop brings together distinguished faculty from both institutions, industry leaders from Taiwan's semiconductor ecosystem, and emerging researchers — all under one roof at NSYSU.

Sessions span cutting-edge topics from advanced packaging and silicon photonics to optical computing and AI-integrated systems, with a unique blend of academic depth and industrial relevance that defines this series.

本次研討會延續 NSYSU 與 PSU 之學術交流精神，以三年為基礎，深化雙邊合作，探討半導體與光電領域之最新突破，並促進產學界的實質對話與合作機會。

Workshop History
2024

1st Edition — Kaohsiung, Taiwan

Inaugural workshop at NSYSU following the PSU–NSYSU MOU signing. 9 PSU professors, NSYSU faculty & industry leaders from TSMC, ASE, and more.

2025

2nd Edition — University Park, Pennsylvania

The workshop moved to Penn State, expanding collaboration and bringing NSYSU researchers to PSU for joint sessions and lab visits.

2026

3rd Edition — Back to Kaohsiung ✦

Returning to NSYSU with expanded scope, deeper industry engagement, and a broadened international speaker lineup.

Workshop Topics

Key Research Themes

01

Advanced Semiconductor Packaging

Heterogeneous integration, chiplet architectures, 3DIC, and wafer-level packaging technologies driving the post-Moore era.

02

Silicon Photonics & Integrated Optics

On-chip optical interconnects, photonic integrated circuits, and high-bandwidth communication for data centers and AI accelerators.

03

Optical Computing & Neuromorphic Systems

Photonic neural networks, optical reservoir computing, and all-optical signal processing for energy-efficient AI inference.

04

Wide-Bandgap & Power Semiconductors

GaN, SiC, and Ga₂O₃ devices for power electronics, RF applications, and high-frequency conversion in electrification and 6G.

05

Photonics Sensing & Imaging

Polarimetric imaging, LiDAR, structured light, and advanced sensing for autonomous vehicles, biomedical, and defense.

06

Metasurfaces & Reconfigurable Optics

Flat optics, active metasurfaces, liquid crystal RIS, and dynamic beam steering for 6G and AR/VR.
